	Happy International Children's Book Day! International Children's Book Day is celebrated annually on April 2 - on the birthday of the Danish author Hans Christian Andersen, who gave us the fairy tales "The Snow Queen" and "The Little Mermaid", among others. During the Dussmann Book Box fundraising campaign last year, we donated books to more than 40 institutions, which care and support sick children in Germany, Austria and Switzerland. Who chooses the books, which go into the Book Box? If the facility has no specific wishes, our experts in the children's book department at Dussmann das KulturKaufhaus GmbH put a selection of books together, which is appropriate to the age of the children, and their situation. For many children, reading stories aloud is an important everyday ritual. Older children love fantasy, which helps them to escape real life and they identify with strong personalities in the stories. Whatever their preferences, we want to make children's lives a little more colorful with the power of literature. We look forward to continuing the Book Box campaign in 2021. And invite everyone, both adults and children, to read books together!

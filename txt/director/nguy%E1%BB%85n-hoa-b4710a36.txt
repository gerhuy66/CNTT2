	Nghe hấp dẫn quá em nhỉ! Lâu quá không có update của em?
	Welcome a new year and hope for a healthy and prosperous 2021! Lights are shining over our journey with the pandemic, still there remain uncertainties. Since the first days of the pandemic, PSI team has strengthened our effort to respond quickly and effectively with support of digital technology. In addition to social behavior change communication campaigns, we help build capacity on data capturing and reporting to aid ongoing disease surveillance for private health facilities. Looking back after 6-month implementation, the project reached 4.3 million people via multi-channels, distributed 60,000 preventive products, and reported nearly 20,000 suspected cases via PSI reporting system. Our achievement is a drop to an ocean, but we are still proud that our effort to initiate private sector engagement in health surveillance has been recognized. Allow me to acknowledge great contribution of PSI’s private sector partners and support from Department of Health as well as CDC in all project provinces.

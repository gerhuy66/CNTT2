Selected Participant in "Net Fluxists" workshop
Company Name
Sàn Art
Dates Employed Jan 2021
Employment Duration 1 mo
Location Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
Happening during the weekends from 16th to 24th January 2021, Net Fluxists is a crash course in Media Art history presented by two facilitators Nguyen Hoang Giang and Chau Tran-Vi. The curriculum is a journey from the
Fluxus movement in the 70s and the concurrent birth of Media Art, to Internet art in the early 90s and to the current age of Post-Internet art. The course is aimed at curators / artists / designers / performers / hackers, etc.

Location Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
CáRô – a five-week arts education programme initiated by Galerie Quynh – provides the skills to understand Contemporary Art with a focus on the local arts scene, grounded in the context of how Modern Art evolved around
the world.
The project aims to supplement arts education for young people and students, aged 13 to 18 who are based in Ho Chi Minh City, having identified a lack of such education within the city and across Vietnam. Instead of solely
focusing on art practice and technical skills, CáRô delivers a curriculum that stimulates formal visual analysis (through both a discursive and embodied means), research skills, and critical thinking.
